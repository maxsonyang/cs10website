Maxson Yang

B.S. Conservation and Resource Studies

Where are you from? What was your path to Cal?

I grew up in a small city called West Covina which is like, 20 miles(???) from Downtown LA. I applied to Berkeley sort of on a whim; I planned to apply to three UCs and was pretty certain about applying to UCI and UCLA; I was left deciding between UCSD and Berkeley. I ended up picking Berkeley because there was less reading involved but now I’m here and it’s great!!

How much programming have you done? What languages?

I've done quite a bit of programming since taking CS10. Languages I use most often include Python, JavaScript, TypeScript, Swift, HTML, and CSS, as well as the framework React. Python's just a really popular coding language that has lots of fun quirks. Swift is the language of the future for iOS development. HTML, CSS, JS, TS, and React, are all commonly used in modern web development! If you like making things, I highly recommend checking all of these out at some point. Other languages I'm familiar with include Java, C, and C++

What are your hobbies?

*cue existential crisis*

Have you done anything remarkable? Has anything memorable happened to you?

I recently won the iHackHealth appathon, and now that's transitioned into a very early-stage startup! I also lost a spelling bee once because I forgot to include the 's' in congratulations.

What commitments will be consuming your cycles this semester?

Building our start-up, doing CS Education Research with Professor Dan Garcia, and teaching! Also trying to pick up some new skills. What skills? Who knows? Who cares?
